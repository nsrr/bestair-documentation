## 5.4 Equipment

**Provided by processing lab:**

* Bedpan, urinal, or specimen hat
* Biohazard bag
* Urine specimen container, label, and barcode
* LabCorp Requisition Form

**Personal Protective Equipment (PPE):**

* Non-sterile gloves
* Lab coat
