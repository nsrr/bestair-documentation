## 7.6.5 Centrifugation

### 7.6.5.1 Plasma

If centrifugation is not immediate, tubes are stored upright on wet ice for up to 30 minutes. Tubes are centrifuged at 4ÂºC at a minimum of 1500g for 10 minutes. Maximum time elapsed before centrifugation is 30 minutes from time of collection.  Once centrifugation is complete, tubes are carefully placed on ice and are ready to aliquot.

### 7.6.5.2. Serum

Allow the serum tube to clot for at least 1 hour at room temperature (maximum time before centrifugation is 90 minutes).  These tubes are centrifuged at room temperature at 1500g for 10 minutes.
